# SharePoint — HR & Operational Policies

## PTO

- Years 1–3: 15 days accrued per year
- Years 4+: 20 days accrued per year
- PTO does not roll over at year end — use it or lose it
- PTO requests must be submitted in JustWorks at least 2 weeks in advance for absences of 3+ days
- Manager approval required for all PTO
- PTO cannot be taken during restricted blackout periods (communicated by management)

## Remote Work

- Up to 2 days per week remote permitted with manager approval
- Compliance and Legal personnel require CCO sign-off for any remote days
- Employees must be reachable and responsive during core hours (9am–5pm local time) when remote
- Remote work privileges can be revoked for performance or conduct reasons
- International remote work (outside the US) requires HR and Legal approval

## Onboarding — New Hire Checklist

New hires must complete all of the following within 30 days of start date:

- Background check clearance (completed before Day 1)
- Form U4 filing with FINRA (where applicable)
- Ethics and compliance training
- Information barrier acknowledgment
- Benefits enrollment in JustWorks
- IT security training
- Direct deposit setup in JustWorks
- Review and sign Employee Handbook

## Termination

- IT access revoked same day as termination
- Building access revoked same day as termination
- Final paycheck issued within 3 business days (varies by state law — confirm with HR)
- COBRA notice issued within 14 days of termination
- Departing employees must return all firm property on last day
- Non-solicitation period: 12 months post-termination
- Non-disclosure obligations: indefinite

## Benefits Enrollment

- Open enrollment: November 1–15 each year; changes take effect January 1
- Changes outside open enrollment require a qualifying life event (marriage, divorce, new child, loss of other coverage)
- Qualifying life events must be reported to HR within 30 days
- Benefits elections are made through JustWorks

## Leave Policies

- FMLA: up to 12 weeks unpaid leave; eligible after 12 months of service
- Parental leave: 8 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 4 weeks paid for secondary caregiver
- Bereavement: 3 days paid for immediate family, 1 day for extended family
- Jury duty: paid for up to 10 business days; documentation required
- Military leave: per USERRA requirements

## Performance Management

- Annual reviews conducted in December
- Mid-year check-ins in June
- Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs) documented in writing and signed by employee
- Promotion decisions announced Q1 each year

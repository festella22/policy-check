# JustWorks — Payroll, Benefits & HR Administration

## Payroll

- Pay cycle: bi-weekly (every other Friday)
- Direct deposit required — no paper checks issued
- Payroll changes (bank account, tax withholding) must be submitted in JustWorks by Wednesday of a pay week to take effect that cycle
- Off-cycle payments require VP-level approval and a $50 processing fee
- Year-end W-2s available in JustWorks by January 31

## Health Insurance

- Firm covers 80% of employee premium
- Firm covers 50% of dependent premium
- Plans available: medical (3 options), dental, vision
- Coverage begins on first day of employment
- Enrollment must be completed in JustWorks within 30 days of hire

## 401(k)

- Employer match: 3% of salary, dollar-for-dollar
- Match is vested immediately (100% immediate vesting)
- Employees can change contribution percentage at any time in JustWorks
- Annual IRS contribution limit applies (updated each January)
- Roth and traditional options available
- Loans against 401(k) balance are not permitted

## FMLA & Leave Administration

- Employees eligible after 12 months of service and 1,250 hours worked
- Up to 12 weeks unpaid, job-protected leave per year
- FMLA requests must be submitted to HR in writing at least 30 days in advance when foreseeable
- Medical certification required within 15 days of request
- Health insurance continues during FMLA leave under the same terms

## State Compliance

- Firm registered as employer in: New York, Connecticut, Florida
- Hiring employees in other states requires HR and Legal approval before offer is made
- State-specific leave laws (NY Paid Family Leave, CT PFMLA, etc.) apply where relevant — contact HR for details
- Remote employees are subject to the employment laws of the state where they work

## Time Off Tracking

- All PTO, sick days, and leaves tracked in JustWorks
- Sick time: 5 days per year (separate from PTO); carries over up to 10 days
- Sick time cannot be used for personal days or vacation
- Holiday schedule published annually in JustWorks

## Expense Reimbursement

- Expense reports submitted through JustWorks
- Reimbursements processed in the next payroll cycle after approval
- Manager approval required for all expense submissions
- Receipts must be attached for expenses over $25 (aligns with Confluence T&E policy)

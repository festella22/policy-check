# Confluence — Regulatory & Compliance Policies

## Travel & Expense

- Expenses must be submitted within 30 days of incurring them
- Receipts required for any expense over $25
- Meals: up to $75/person for client entertainment; $35/person for internal
- Travel must be booked through the firm's approved travel portal
- Personal car mileage reimbursed at the current IRS rate
- No first-class travel without Managing Director approval

## Conflicts of Interest

- All outside business activities must be disclosed to Compliance within 5 business days of starting them
- Personal investments in covered securities require pre-clearance from Compliance
- Gifts received over $100 in value must be reported to Compliance
- Gifts given over $100 require prior Compliance approval
- Romantic relationships with direct reports must be disclosed to HR immediately

## Information Barrier Policy

- Personnel on restricted lists may not share material non-public information (MNPI) with anyone outside their barrier
- Crossing an information barrier requires written approval from Compliance and Legal
- Information barrier breaches must be self-reported to Compliance within 24 hours
- Physical and electronic barriers must be maintained between restricted groups

## Code of Ethics

- Annual certification required — due by January 31 each year
- Violations must be self-reported to Compliance within 48 hours of discovery
- Retaliation against anyone who reports a violation is strictly prohibited
- All employees are required to complete annual compliance training

## Record Retention

- Client records: retained 7 years
- Trade records: retained 5 years per SEC Rule 17a-4
- Email communications related to client matters: retained 7 years
- HR records: retained 7 years after termination
- Financial records: retained 7 years
- Records must not be destroyed once a legal hold has been issued

## Regulatory Filings

- Form U4 must be updated within 30 days of any reportable event
- Outside business activities reported on U4 must be pre-approved by Compliance
- Annual Form U5 amendments require Compliance review before submission

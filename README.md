# Policy Check

Instant answers from internal policy and regulatory guidance.

Covers Confluence (compliance & regulatory), SharePoint (HR & operations), and JustWorks (payroll & benefits).

## Usage

Ask any policy question naturally:
- "What's the expense receipt threshold?"
- "How many PTO days do I get in year 2?"
- "What do I need to disclose for a conflict of interest?"
- "How does the 401k match work?"
- "What happens to my benefits if I'm terminated?"

## Updating policy data

Replace the content in `skills/policy-check/references/` with exports from your actual systems:
- `confluence-policies.md` — paste from Confluence
- `sharepoint-hr.md` — paste from SharePoint
- `justworks-payroll.md` — paste from JustWorks
